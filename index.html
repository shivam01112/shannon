About Us

We are Shannon Advisors, a SEBI-registered Category I Merchant Banker founded in 2018 with a focused approach to India’s equity capital markets. We work closely with companies on Mainboard and SME IPOs, guiding them from strategy through to successful listing.

Led by a Chartered Accountant founder-director duo and backed by a team of 20+ experienced professionals, we bring deep market knowledge, regulatory clarity, and execution strength to every mandate.

Our services

Strategic Fundraising

End-to-end advisory on IPOs and Pre-IPO fundraising — including structuring, positioning, regulatory interface, and execution across Mainboard and SME platforms.

Valuation Services

Comprehensive valuation of businesses, equity shares, and assets, backed by in-depth analysis, regulatory compliance, and transaction-grade insight.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Buy-side and sell-side transactions including deal structuring, valuation, due diligence support, and end-to-end execution across private and listed spaces.

Corporate Restructuring & Advisory

Reorganisation strategies including hive-offs, mergers, demergers, and promoter realignments — with regulatory, tax, and shareholder considerations
